## Slide 5 — Verus: 8 Years of Building

8 years ago, Verus launched as a protocol and blockchain network.

No pre-allocation, no ICO, no presale — just a vision of people-powered public infrastructure networks.

Our first step was to resolve the **blockchain trilemma** (scalability, decentralization, security). We invented **Public Blockchains as a Service (PBaaS)**.

PBaaS enables anyone to create their own public or private blockchain, provably connected to the Verus network, operated by people worldwide.

## Slide 6 — Rent-Free Protocol

Verus is not just decentralized — it is **rent-free**.

You can:
- Launch your own public network

No third parties, no companies, no middlemen extracting rent. All fees are shared fairly between contributors to the protocol.

## Slide 7 — Self-Sovereign Identity

**VerusID** is the bedrock that enables you to control your own identity, data, and assets.

- Rent-free, permanent, worldwide-resolvable IDs
- Each identity has an indexed key/value database with built-in encryption
- Post-quantum encrypted password manager built in
- 2-factor authentication by default
- Available on Web, Desktop, and mobile
- **Agent-native** — powerful for enabling AI agents

## Slide 9 — Privacy by Default

Data that any DREAM app creates is stored in your VerusID database, **encrypted by default**.

- Ensures privacy, control, and security
- Public data is encrypted with a published key
- Enables regulators to publish "do-not-decrypt lists" for jurisdictional content regulation
- No authoritarian, omnipotent censorship

## Slide 10 — No MEV Extraction

**Basket currencies** provide liquidity with built-in conversion.

Between January 2024 and April 2025, over 2.8 million Ethereum blocks included MEV attacks (front-running, back-running, sandwich attacks). This extracted **~$700M from users**.

DREAM apps have **none of that**:
- All conversions solved simultaneously as one bundle
- Everyone gets the same price in all directions
- No space for MEV bots or block producers to exploit users

## Slide 12 — Legacy vs DREAM Architecture

**Legacy applications:**
- Client tier (presentation)
- Middle tier (server-side logic + databases)
- Company-controlled middleware between user and data
- Payment processor (2-4% fee + data selling)
- Invasive age verification (facial recognition)

**DREAM redefines the data tier** — blockchain as a public, permanent, user-controlled encrypted database.

## Slide 14 — DREAM Components

DREAM adds critical new components:

- **Self-sovereign, provable IDs**
- **Encrypted data** that IDs can unlock
- **Signatures, payments, and currencies**
- **Common user approval interface** — consistent across all DREAM apps
  - Appears only when necessary
  - Describes clearly what is happening and what it means
  - **Phishing-resistant** — prevents users from being fooled into agreeing to things they don't understand
